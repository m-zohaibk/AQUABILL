# **App Name**: AquaBill

## Core Features:

- Customer Management: Add, edit, and delete customer information, including name and contact details.
- Invoice Generation: Create invoices with date, start time, end time, and rate per minute.
- Automatic Calculation: Automatically calculate the duration and total cost of water supply based on the rate per minute.
- Payment Tracking: Track the amount received and the amount pending for each invoice.
- Reporting and History: Generate reports of customer invoice history, including the ability to export as a PDF.
- Settings Management: Manage settings such as rate per minute, business name, contact information, and address.
- Settings Recommendations: Suggest optimal rate-per-minute based on seasonal demand and regional pricing data, acting as a helpful tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky Blue (#87CEEB) to represent water and clarity.
- Background color: Light Blue (#E0FFFF), a very pale tint of the primary.
- Accent color: Cyan (#00FFFF) for interactive elements and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern yet accessible look.
- Use simple, clear icons related to water, time, and money.
- A clean, card-based layout for displaying information and actions.
- Subtle animations for loading states and transitions to improve user experience.
